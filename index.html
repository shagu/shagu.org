About


Long-time Linux user and developer who spent years building World of Warcraft add-ons that reached hundreds of thousands of players. I create and maintain several open-source projects - from Magic: The Gathering collection management to lightweight game development libraries. Beyond coding, I configure and update my homelab with Docker Compose and LXC, design custom parts in FreeCAD, and bring them to life via 3D printing. I'm also passionate about retro emulation handheld devices. For a living, I build embedded Linux distributions with Yocto, where I track down and patch security flaws in these systems.
